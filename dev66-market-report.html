Opportunity Analysis

6 gaps Dev66
can fill

These are the real unmet needs in Pakistan's data education market right now — your competitive advantages.

🗣️
Bilingual Teaching (Urdu + English)
Most premium bootcamps teach in English only. Students (16–22) from Tier-2 cities struggle. Dev66 in Hinglish = massive reach advantage.
💼
Real Portfolio Projects
Free courses give zero real-world projects. Atomcamp has some but very expensive. Dev66 can include 2–3 industry-style capstone projects per course.
🤝
Small Cohorts = Real Mentorship
PIAIC/SMIT have hundreds of students per batch — zero individual attention. Dev66 with 15–25 students per cohort = direct teacher access, which students will pay for.
💳
Affordable with Installments
Nobody in the mid-range offers installment plans. This removes the #1 barrier for students (16–22) and fresh grads whose families can't pay a lump sum.
🎯
Job-Ready Curriculum (Not Theory)
Most courses are theory-heavy. Build your curriculum around what employers actually ask in Pakistani job interviews — SQL, Power BI, Python pandas, basic ML.
🌍
Remote Students from Small Cities
Most quality bootcamps are Karachi/Lahore/Islamabad centric. A fully online, Urdu-friendly school can reach Multan, Faisalabad, Peshawar, Quetta — untapped market.

Course Structure

Suggested course
timeline (5 Months)

A practical, job-focused curriculum designed for students aged 16–22 with zero prior experience.

Month 1
Foundation — Python & Data Basics

Zero to hero. No prior coding needed. Build confidence early.

  • Python basics (variables, loops, functions)
  • Google Colab setup
  • Excel fundamentals
  • Data types & structures
Month 2
SQL — The #1 Analyst Skill

Every data job in Pakistan requires SQL. Make students job-ready here.

  • MySQL / PostgreSQL
  • SELECT, JOIN, GROUP BY
  • Real database projects
  • Mini assignment: sales analysis
Month 3
Python for Data (Pandas & Visualization)

Hands-on data manipulation and storytelling with charts.

  • Pandas (DataFrames, cleaning)
  • Matplotlib & Seaborn
  • EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis)
  • Mini project: customer data
Month 4
Power BI — Dashboard & Reporting

The most in-demand tool in Pakistani corporate jobs right now.

  • Power BI Desktop
  • DAX basics
  • Building dashboards
  • Connecting to SQL + Excel
Month 5
Capstone Project + Career Prep

Real project for portfolio. Job hunt support. Graduation.

  • End-to-end industry project
  • LinkedIn profile review
  • Resume building workshop
  • Mock interview prep
  • Certificate ceremony
